Zero Parameters Festival

Sunday 10th to Sunday 17th of June, 2022. || Dai Hall, Unit 29/30, The Piazza, Huddersfield, HD1 2RS

Pre-celebrating KYOM 2023, A week-length festival, Zero Parameters tells stories of diverse performance art & culture. We have commissioned marginal and experimental artists and musicians from all over the world to do residencies, an exhibition, live music, graffiti workshops and digital skill share projects, all presenting their works from the heart of Kirklees, dai hall.
